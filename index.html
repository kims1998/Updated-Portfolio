About Me

Hello, I am Charles Kim! I am 23 years old. I am a Marine Corps Veteran currently working as a On-site Quick Response Team Member. In the military I was working on the MV-22. I spent 5 years in the Marine Corps being stationed in Okinawa, JP to San Diego, CA. I deployed to the East China Sea in 2018 and to Kuwait back in late 2020. One of my biggest life mottos I live by is "If not me, then who?" This quote came from a Iraq War Veteran's story in 2007. Since I heard about that quote, I lived by it and applied it to everything. My hobbies include going to the gym, playing video games, and now coding! I decided to become a Web Developer due to my current career coming to a halt.
